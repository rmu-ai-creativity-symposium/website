About the Symposium

The AI and Creativity Symposium brings together researchers from the three RMU universities to examine how emerging AI systems intersect with human imagination. Sessions will mix invited talks, demonstrations, and collaborative discussions spanning generative models, cognitive neuroscience of creativity, human-AI co-creation workflows, and ethical considerations. A closing panel will synthesize perspectives and invite audience dialogue on the future of creative practice with AI in the loop.
